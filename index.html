ABOUT THE INVESTIGATORS

Jonathan Riehle is a mystery enthusiast and independent researcher focused on unexplained stories, archived reports, and unsolved investigations explored from a home-based perspective.

Jennifer Riehle serves as co-host and research partner — helping compare theories, analyze evidence, and challenge assumptions through open-ended discussion.

Bonded in Mystery is not a professional investigation agency. It is a curiosity-driven podcast project built around conversation, exploration, and the search for answers hidden within the unknown.

DISCUSSION FORMAT

Every case is explored through conversation, theory comparison, archived evidence, witness testimony, and speculative debate.

No claims are presented as definitive fact — only possibilities, interpretations, and the ongoing pursuit of understanding.
